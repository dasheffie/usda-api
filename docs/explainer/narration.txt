Thirteen skills. One API. This repo wraps the USDA FoodData Central nutrition database into drop-in abilities for any agent runtime. Ask it the lowest sodium bowl at Chipotle under seven hundred calories. Scan a cereal box for hypertension red flags. Tally a day's added sugar. Swap two items in your cart to stay under a threshold. Each skill is a single markdown file, a name, a description, instructions, examples. Your agent loads it like any other skill. Every one of these was trained. Three roles. One model answers. A second counts issues. A third proposes edits. Never the same role twice. Three rounds. A hundred held out questions per skill. The trajectories converged. Three skills shipped clean. Seven plateaued inside tolerance. Three missed the bar, marked, but still working. Read the skill dot M D. Fork it. Retrain it. It's M I T.
